ABOUT US

Providing Our Guest with Unforgettable Experiences to Cherish forever after

Cradled in peaceful surroundings, our resort is a serene retreat where lush paddy fields and beautiful vistas create a tranquil backdrop. Experience a harmonious blend of comfort and nature’s splendour, with each stay offering amazing vistas and a peaceful ambience. Unwind and rejuvenate in our spell-binding haven, where relaxation meets natural splendour. Lindy Stays Charming 5-Bedroom Villa is located in the calm town of Thalpe, about 2 kilometres from Thalpe Beach and 2.5 kilometres from Koggala Beach. This modern property has a pool with breathtaking views of paddy fields, a garden, and free parking for guests. Each air-conditioned room has a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, a wardrobe, and a private bathroom; some rooms include a terrace for additional recreation. Guests can delight in a buffet breakfast, a fully equipped open kitchen, and laundry facilities. Key sites like Galle Fort and Galle Lighthouse can be found 13-14 km away, while Koggala Airport is approximately 4 km from the property. Lindy Stays provides a pleasant and scenic getaway ideal for relaxation and discovery
